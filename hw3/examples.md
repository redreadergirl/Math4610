## Concurrent Computing Examples:

**Matrix multiplied by matrix:**

Input:

    mmomp({{1, 2, 3}, {3, 7, 5}}, {{4, 6}, {4, 2}, {9, -4}})
    
Output:

    {{39, -2}, {85, 12}}
    
**Matrix multiplied by vector:**

Input:

    mvomp({{2, 6, 2}, {4, 7, 3}, {3, -2, -5}}, {1, 4, -2})
    
Output:

    {22, 26, 5}
